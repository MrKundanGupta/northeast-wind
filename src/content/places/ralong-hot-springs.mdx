- **By Air:** Pakyong Airport, Gangtok (PYG) — approximately 33 km (57 mins drive)
- **By Air:** Bagdogra Airport (IXB) — approximately 96 km (165 mins drive)
- **By Train:** New Jalpaiguri Railway Station (NJP) — approximately 93 km (159 mins drive)

## About Lady Hydari Park

Lady Hydari Park is a popular **Wildlife & Nature** destination located in **Meghalaya**, Northeast India. Categorized as *Nature Reserve*, it draws visitors looking for an authentic Northeast Indian experience.

## Entry Fees

| Visitor Type | Fee |
|---|---|
| Indian Nationals | ₹100 - ₹500 |
| Foreign Nationals | ₹500 - ₹2000 |

## How to Reach

- **By Air:** Umroi Airport, Shillong (SHL) — approximately 23 km (39 mins drive)
- **By Air:** Lokpriya Gopinath Bordoloi International Airport, Guwahati (GAU) — approximately 93 km (159 mins drive)
- **By Train:** Guwahati Railway Station — approximately 96 km (165 mins drive)

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **October, November, December, January, February, March, April, May**.

### Events & Festivals

- Shillong Cherry Blossom Festival (Nov)
- Wangala Festival (Nov)
- Nongkrem Dance (Nov)

## Visiting Hours

- **Opens:** 06:00 AM
- **Closes:** 04:30 PM
- **Closed on:** Varies by season
- **Note:** Entry may close 1-2 hours before official closing. Closed during monsoon in some parks.

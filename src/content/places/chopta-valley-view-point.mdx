## About Chopta Valley View Point

Chopta Valley View Point is a popular **Viewpoint / Passes** destination located in **Sikkim**, Northeast India. Categorized as *Scenic Viewpoint*, it draws visitors looking for an authentic Northeast Indian experience.

## Entry Fees

| Visitor Type | Fee |
|---|---|
| Indian Nationals | Free |
| Foreign Nationals | Free |

## How to Reach

- **By Air:** Pakyong Airport, Gangtok (PYG) — approximately 73 km (125 mins drive)
- **By Air:** Bagdogra Airport (IXB) — approximately 165 km (283 mins drive)
- **By Train:** New Jalpaiguri Railway Station (NJP) — approximately 157 km (269 mins drive)

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **March, April, May, October, November, December**.

### Events & Festivals

- Losar (Feb-Mar)
- Saga Dawa (May-Jun)
- Pang Lhabsol (Aug-Sep)

## Visiting Hours

- **Opens:** 06:00 AM
- **Closes:** 06:00 PM
- **Note:** Best visited during clear weather for panoramic views. Sunrise and sunset are recommended.

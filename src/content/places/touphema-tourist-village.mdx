## About Touphema Tourist Village

Touphema Tourist Village is a popular **Home Stay** destination located in **Nagaland**, Northeast India. Categorized as *Homestay*, it draws visitors looking for an authentic Northeast Indian experience.

## How to Reach

- **By Air:** Dimapur Airport (DMU) — approximately 58 km (99 mins drive)
- **By Train:** Dimapur Railway Station — approximately 62 km (106 mins drive)

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **October, November, December, January, February, March**.

### Events & Festivals

- Hornbill Festival (Dec)
- Moatsu (May)
- Sekrenyi (Feb)

## Permit Requirements

Inner Line Permit (ILP) required for Indian citizens (Dimapur is exempt). Restricted Area Permit (RAP) for foreign nationals. Apply at Nagaland House in major cities or online.

## About Zeilad Lake

Zeilad Lake is a popular **Lake / Nature** destination located in **Manipur**, Northeast India. Categorized as *Lake*, it draws visitors looking for an authentic Northeast Indian experience.

## Entry Fees

| Visitor Type | Fee |
|---|---|
| Indian Nationals | Free / ₹50 |
| Foreign Nationals | Free / ₹100 |

## How to Reach

- **By Air:** Bir Tikendrajit International Airport, Imphal (IMF) — approximately 72 km (123 mins drive)
- **By Train:** Jiribam Railway Station — approximately 54 km (93 mins drive)

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **October, November, December, January, February, March, April**.

### Events & Festivals

- Sangai Festival (Nov)
- Yaoshang (Mar)
- Lui Ngai Ni (Feb)

## Visiting Hours

- **Opens:** 06:00 AM
- **Closes:** 06:00 PM
- **Note:** Boating hours may differ. Best visited in early morning or late afternoon.

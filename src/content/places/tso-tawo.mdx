## About Tso-Tawo (Spirit Stone)

Tso-Tawo (Spirit Stone) is a popular **Heritage** destination located in **Nagaland**, Northeast India. Categorized as *Historical Monument*, it draws visitors looking for an authentic Northeast Indian experience.

## Entry Fees

| Visitor Type | Fee |
|---|---|
| Indian Nationals | ₹20 - ₹50 |
| Foreign Nationals | ₹200 - ₹500 |

## How to Reach

- **By Air:** Dimapur Airport (DMU) — approximately 76 km (130 mins drive)
- **By Train:** Dimapur Railway Station — approximately 78 km (134 mins drive)

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **October, November, December, January, February, March**.

### Events & Festivals

- Hornbill Festival (Dec)
- Moatsu (May)
- Sekrenyi (Feb)

## Permit Requirements

Inner Line Permit (ILP) required for Indian citizens (Dimapur is exempt). Restricted Area Permit (RAP) for foreign nationals. Apply at Nagaland House in major cities or online.

## Visiting Hours

- **Opens:** 09:00 AM
- **Closes:** 05:00 PM
- **Closed on:** Monday
- **Note:** Some heritage sites close on national holidays.

## About Moopun Falls

Moopun Falls is a popular **Waterfall** destination located in **Meghalaya**, Northeast India. Categorized as *Waterfall*, it draws visitors looking for an authentic Northeast Indian experience.

## Entry Fees

| Visitor Type | Fee |
|---|---|
| Indian Nationals | Free / ₹20 |
| Foreign Nationals | Free / ₹50 |

## How to Reach

- **By Air:** Umroi Airport, Shillong (SHL) — approximately 63 km (108 mins drive)
- **By Air:** Lokpriya Gopinath Bordoloi International Airport, Guwahati (GAU) — approximately 120 km (206 mins drive)
- **By Train:** Guwahati Railway Station — approximately 130 km (223 mins drive)

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **October, November, December, January, February, March, April, May**.

### Events & Festivals

- Shillong Cherry Blossom Festival (Nov)
- Wangala Festival (Nov)
- Nongkrem Dance (Nov)
- Monsoon season (Jul-Sep) for maximum water flow

## Visiting Hours

- **Opens:** 06:00 AM
- **Closes:** 05:00 PM
- **Note:** Best visited during morning hours. May be inaccessible during heavy monsoon.

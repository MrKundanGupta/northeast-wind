## About Nagaland State Museum

Nagaland State Museum is a popular **Museum** destination located in **Nagaland**, Northeast India. Categorized as *Museum*, it draws visitors looking for an authentic Northeast Indian experience.

## Entry Fees

| Visitor Type | Fee |
|---|---|
| Indian Nationals | ₹20 - ₹50 |
| Foreign Nationals | ₹100 - ₹300 |

## How to Reach

- **By Air:** Dimapur Airport (DMU) — approximately 57 km (98 mins drive)
- **By Train:** Dimapur Railway Station — approximately 62 km (106 mins drive)

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **October, November, December, January, February, March**.

### Events & Festivals

- Hornbill Festival (Dec)
- Moatsu (May)
- Sekrenyi (Feb)

## Permit Requirements

Inner Line Permit (ILP) required for Indian citizens (Dimapur is exempt). Restricted Area Permit (RAP) for foreign nationals. Apply at Nagaland House in major cities or online.

## Visiting Hours

- **Opens:** 10:00 AM
- **Closes:** 04:30 PM
- **Closed on:** Monday, National Holidays
- **Note:** Last entry typically 30 mins before closing.

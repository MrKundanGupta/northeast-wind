## About Ngengpui Wildlife Sanctuary

Ngengpui Wildlife Sanctuary is a popular **Wildlife & Nature** destination located in **Mizoram**, Northeast India. Categorized as *Wildlife Sanctuary*, it draws visitors looking for an authentic Northeast Indian experience.

## Entry Fees

| Visitor Type | Fee |
|---|---|
| Indian Nationals | ₹100 - ₹500 |
| Foreign Nationals | ₹500 - ₹2000 |

> Separate charges for camera, vehicle, and guide. Prices vary by zone and vehicle type.

## How to Reach

- **By Air:** Lengpui Airport, Aizawl (AJL) — approximately 298 km (511 mins drive)
- **By Train:** Bairabi Railway Station — approximately 379 km (650 mins drive)

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **October, November, December, January, February, March**.

### Events & Festivals

- Chapchar Kut (Mar)
- Mim Kut (Aug-Sep)
- Anthurium Festival (Sep)

## Permit Requirements

Inner Line Permit (ILP) required for Indian citizens. Apply online or at Liaison Officer offices in Kolkata, Delhi, Guwahati, or Silchar. Foreign nationals need a Restricted Area Permit.

## Visiting Hours

- **Opens:** 06:00 AM
- **Closes:** 04:30 PM
- **Closed on:** Varies by season
- **Note:** Entry may close 1-2 hours before official closing. Closed during monsoon in some parks.

## How to Reach

- **By Air:** Umroi Airport, Shillong (SHL) — approximately 75 km (129 mins drive)
- **By Air:** Lokpriya Gopinath Bordoloi International Airport, Guwahati (GAU) — approximately 129 km (221 mins drive)
- **By Train:** Guwahati Railway Station — approximately 140 km (240 mins drive)

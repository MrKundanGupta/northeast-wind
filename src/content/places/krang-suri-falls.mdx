## About Krang Suri Falls

Krang Suri Falls in the Jaintia Hills is one of Meghalaya's most photogenic waterfalls — turquoise water framed by dark cliffs and lush subtropical forest. The two-tier falls feed a swimming pool of extraordinary colour, accessible by a short forest walk. Less crowded than Cherrapunji's famous falls, this is a true hidden gem.

## How to Reach

- **From Jowai:** Approximately 30 km (50 minutes by road)


## Best Time to Visit

Ideal months: **October, November, December, January, February, March**

## About Deopahar Ruins

Deopahar is an 8th–12th century archaeological site near Numaligarh featuring clusters of ruined Hindu and Buddhist temples scattered across a forested hill. The site preserves beautiful stone carvings of Vishnu, Shiva, and tantric goddesses, largely unvisited by tourists — making it one of Assam's most atmospheric hidden heritage discoveries.

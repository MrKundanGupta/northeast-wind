## About Wei Sawdong Falls

Wei Sawdong is a stunning three-tiered waterfall tucked in the forest near Cherrapunji, relatively unknown and less visited than Nohkalikai. The lower tiers have natural pools perfect for swimming, accessible via a short jungle trek. Post-monsoon (October–February) offers crystal-clear water in a lush green setting.

## How to Reach

- **From Cherrapunji:** Approximately 15 km (30 minutes by road)


## Best Time to Visit

Ideal months: **October, November, December, January, February**

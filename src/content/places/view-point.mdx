## About View point

View point is a popular **Viewpoint / Passes** destination located in **Sikkim**, Northeast India. Categorized as *Scenic Viewpoint*, it draws visitors looking for an authentic Northeast Indian experience.

| Visitor Type | Fee |
|---|---|
| Indian Nationals | Free |
| Foreign Nationals | Free |

## How to Reach

- **By Air:** Pakyong Airport, Gangtok (PYG) — approximately 469 km (804 mins drive)
- **By Air:** Bagdogra Airport (IXB) — approximately 482 km (826 mins drive)
- **By Train:** New Jalpaiguri Railway Station (NJP) — approximately 468 km (802 mins drive)

The ideal months to visit are **March, April, May, October, November, December**.

## About SCIENCE MUSEUM, Khanapara Guwahati

SCIENCE MUSEUM, Khanapara Guwahati is a popular **Museum** destination located in **Assam**, Northeast India. Categorized as *Museum*, it draws visitors looking for an authentic Northeast Indian experience.

## Entry Fees

| Visitor Type | Fee |
|---|---|
| Indian Nationals | ₹20 - ₹50 |
| Foreign Nationals | ₹100 - ₹300 |

## How to Reach

- **By Air:** Lokpriya Gopinath Bordoloi International Airport, Guwahati (GAU) — approximately 30 km (51 mins drive)
- **By Train:** Guwahati Railway Station — approximately 12 km (21 mins drive)

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **October, November, December, January, February, March, April**.

- Ambubachi Mela (Jun)
- Kaziranga Elephant Festival (Feb)

## About Eaglenest Wildlife Sanctuary

Eaglenest Wildlife Sanctuary is a popular **Wildlife & Nature** destination located in **Arunachal Pradesh**, Northeast India. Categorized as *Wildlife Sanctuary*, it draws visitors looking for an authentic Northeast Indian experience.

## Entry Fees

| Visitor Type | Fee |
|---|---|
| Indian Nationals | ₹100 - ₹500 |
| Foreign Nationals | ₹500 - ₹2000 |

> Separate charges for camera, vehicle, and guide. Prices vary by zone and vehicle type.

## How to Reach

- **By Air:** Donyi Polo Airport, Itanagar (HGI) — approximately 175 km (300 mins drive)
- **By Air:** Tezpur Airport (TEZ) — approximately 76 km (130 mins drive)
- **By Train:** Naharlagun Railway Station — approximately 176 km (302 mins drive)

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **October, November, December, January, February, March, April**.

### Events & Festivals

- Losar Festival (Feb-Mar)
- Torgya Festival (Jan)
- Ziro Music Festival (Sep)

## Permit Requirements

Inner Line Permit (ILP) required for Indian citizens. Protected Area Permit (PAP) required for foreign nationals. Apply online at arunachalilp.com or through registered tour operators.

## Visiting Hours

- **Opens:** 06:00 AM
- **Closes:** 04:30 PM
- **Closed on:** Varies by season
- **Note:** Entry may close 1-2 hours before official closing. Closed during monsoon in some parks.

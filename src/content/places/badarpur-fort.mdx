## About Badarpur Fort

Badarpur Fort is a popular **Heritage** destination located in **Assam**, Northeast India. Categorized as *Historical Monument*, it draws visitors looking for an authentic Northeast Indian experience.

## Entry Fees

| Visitor Type | Fee |
|---|---|
| Indian Nationals | ₹20 - ₹50 |
| Foreign Nationals | ₹200 - ₹500 |

## How to Reach

- **By Air:** Lokpriya Gopinath Bordoloi International Airport, Guwahati (GAU) — approximately 239 km (410 mins drive)
- **By Train:** Guwahati Railway Station — approximately 236 km (405 mins drive)

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **October, November, December, January, February, March, April**.

### Events & Festivals

- Bihu Festival (Apr, Oct, Jan)
- Ambubachi Mela (Jun)
- Kaziranga Elephant Festival (Feb)

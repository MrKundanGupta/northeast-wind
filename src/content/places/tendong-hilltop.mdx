## About Tendong Hilltop

Tendong Hilltop is a popular **Viewpoint / Passes** destination located in **Sikkim**, Northeast India. Categorized as *Scenic Viewpoint*, it draws visitors looking for an authentic Northeast Indian experience.

## Entry Fees

| Visitor Type | Fee |
|---|---|
| Indian Nationals | Free |
| Foreign Nationals | Free |

## How to Reach

- **By Air:** Pakyong Airport, Gangtok (PYG) — approximately 26 km (45 mins drive)
- **By Air:** Bagdogra Airport (IXB) — approximately 84 km (144 mins drive)
- **By Train:** New Jalpaiguri Railway Station (NJP) — approximately 80 km (137 mins drive)

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **March, April, May, October, November, December**.

## About Shri Jagannath Temple, Agartala

Shri Jagannath Temple, Agartala is a popular **Spiritual** destination located in **Tripura**, Northeast India. Categorized as *Hindu Temple*, it draws visitors looking for an authentic Northeast Indian experience.

## Entry Fees

| Visitor Type | Fee |
|---|---|
| Indian Nationals | Free |
| Foreign Nationals | Free |

> Photography may have additional charges inside the premises.

## How to Reach

- **By Air:** Maharaja Bir Bikram Airport, Agartala (IXA) — approximately 11 km (19 mins drive)
- **By Train:** Agartala Railway Station — approximately 3 km (10 mins drive)

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **October, November, December, January, February, March**.

### Events & Festivals

- Kharchi Puja (Jul)
- Garia Puja (Apr)
- Orange Festival (Nov)

## Visiting Hours

- **Opens:** 05:00 AM
- **Closes:** 06:00 PM
- **Note:** May close briefly in afternoon for rituals. Dress modestly.

## About Rabdentse Ruins

Rabdentse Ruins is a popular **Heritage** destination located in **Sikkim**, Northeast India. Categorized as *Historical Monument*, it draws visitors looking for an authentic Northeast Indian experience.

- **By Air:** Pakyong Airport, Gangtok (PYG) — approximately 49 km (84 mins drive)
- **By Air:** Bagdogra Airport (IXB) — approximately 96 km (165 mins drive)
- **By Train:** New Jalpaiguri Railway Station (NJP) — approximately 95 km (163 mins drive)

## About Shaheed Minar

Shaheed Minar is a popular **Heritage** destination located in **Manipur**, Northeast India. Categorized as *Historical Monument*, it draws visitors looking for an authentic Northeast Indian experience.

## Entry Fees

| Visitor Type | Fee |
|---|---|
| Indian Nationals | ₹20 - ₹50 |
| Foreign Nationals | ₹200 - ₹500 |

## How to Reach

- **By Air:** Bir Tikendrajit International Airport, Imphal (IMF) — approximately 11 km (19 mins drive)
- **By Train:** Jiribam Railway Station — approximately 115 km (197 mins drive)

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **October, November, December, January, February, March, April**.

### Events & Festivals

- Sangai Festival (Nov)
- Yaoshang (Mar)
- Lui Ngai Ni (Feb)

- **Opens:** 09:00 AM
- **Closes:** 05:00 PM

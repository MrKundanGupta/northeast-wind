## About Dibru Saikhowa National Park

Dibru Saikhowa National Park is a popular **Wildlife & Nature** destination located in **Assam**, Northeast India. Categorized as *National Park*, it draws visitors looking for an authentic Northeast Indian experience.

## Entry Fees

| Visitor Type | Fee |
|---|---|
| Indian Nationals | ₹100 - ₹500 |
| Foreign Nationals | ₹500 - ₹2000 |

> Separate charges for camera, vehicle, and guide. Prices vary by zone and vehicle type.

## How to Reach

- **By Air:** Lokpriya Gopinath Bordoloi International Airport, Guwahati (GAU) — approximately 567 km (972 mins drive)
- **By Train:** Guwahati Railway Station — approximately 541 km (927 mins drive)

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **October, November, December, January, February, March, April**.

### Events & Festivals

- Bihu Festival (Apr, Oct, Jan)
- Ambubachi Mela (Jun)
- Kaziranga Elephant Festival (Feb)

## Visiting Hours

- **Opens:** 06:00 AM
- **Closes:** 04:30 PM
- **Closed on:** Varies by season
- **Note:** Entry may close 1-2 hours before official closing. Closed during monsoon in some parks.

## About Bamuni Hills

Bamuni Hills is a popular **Viewpoint / Passes** destination located in **Assam**, Northeast India. Categorized as *Scenic Viewpoint*, it draws visitors looking for an authentic Northeast Indian experience.

## Entry Fees

| Visitor Type | Fee |
|---|---|
| Indian Nationals | Free |
| Foreign Nationals | Free |

## How to Reach

- **By Air:** Lokpriya Gopinath Bordoloi International Airport, Guwahati (GAU) — approximately 186 km (319 mins drive)
- **By Train:** Guwahati Railway Station — approximately 160 km (274 mins drive)

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **October, November, December, January, February, March, April**.

### Events & Festivals

- Bihu Festival (Apr, Oct, Jan)
- Ambubachi Mela (Jun)
- Kaziranga Elephant Festival (Feb)

## Visiting Hours

- **Opens:** 06:00 AM
- **Closes:** 06:00 PM
- **Note:** Best visited during clear weather for panoramic views. Sunrise and sunset are recommended.

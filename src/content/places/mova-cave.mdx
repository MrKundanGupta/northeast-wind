## About Mova Cave

Mova Cave is a popular **Caving / Adventure** destination located in **Manipur**, Northeast India. Categorized as *Cave Exploration*, it draws visitors looking for an authentic Northeast Indian experience.

## How to Reach

- **By Air:** Bir Tikendrajit International Airport, Imphal (IMF) — approximately 78 km (134 mins drive)
- **By Train:** Jiribam Railway Station — approximately 173 km (297 mins drive)

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **October, November, December, January, February, March, April**.

- **Opens:** 09:00 AM

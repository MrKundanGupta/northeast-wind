## About Phamrong Falls

Phamrong Falls is a popular **Waterfall** destination located in **Sikkim**, Northeast India. Categorized as *Waterfall*, it draws visitors looking for an authentic Northeast Indian experience.

## Entry Fees

| Visitor Type | Fee |
|---|---|
| Indian Nationals | Free / ₹20 |
| Foreign Nationals | Free / ₹50 |

## How to Reach

- **By Air:** Pakyong Airport, Gangtok (PYG) — approximately 33 km (57 mins drive)
- **By Air:** Bagdogra Airport (IXB) — approximately 89 km (153 mins drive)
- **By Train:** New Jalpaiguri Railway Station (NJP) — approximately 86 km (147 mins drive)

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **March, April, May, October, November, December**.

### Events & Festivals

- Losar (Feb-Mar)
- Saga Dawa (May-Jun)
- Pang Lhabsol (Aug-Sep)
- Monsoon season (Jul-Sep) for maximum water flow

## Visiting Hours

- **Opens:** 06:00 AM
- **Closes:** 05:00 PM
- **Note:** Best visited during morning hours. May be inaccessible during heavy monsoon.

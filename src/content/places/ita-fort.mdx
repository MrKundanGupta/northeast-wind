## About Ita Fort

Ita Fort is a popular **Heritage** destination located in **Arunachal Pradesh**, Northeast India. Categorized as *Historical Monument*, it draws visitors looking for an authentic Northeast Indian experience.

## Entry Fees

| Visitor Type | Fee |
|---|---|
| Indian Nationals | ₹20 - ₹50 |
| Foreign Nationals | ₹200 - ₹500 |

## How to Reach

- **By Air:** Donyi Polo Airport, Itanagar (HGI) — approximately 11 km (19 mins drive)
- **By Air:** Tezpur Airport (TEZ) — approximately 128 km (219 mins drive)
- **By Train:** Naharlagun Railway Station — approximately 12 km (21 mins drive)

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **October, November, December, January, February, March, April**.

### Events & Festivals

- Losar Festival (Feb-Mar)
- Torgya Festival (Jan)
- Ziro Music Festival (Sep)

## Permit Requirements

Inner Line Permit (ILP) required for Indian citizens. Protected Area Permit (PAP) required for foreign nationals. Apply online at arunachalilp.com or through registered tour operators.

## Visiting Hours

- **Opens:** 09:00 AM
- **Closes:** 05:00 PM
- **Closed on:** Monday
- **Note:** Some heritage sites close on national holidays.

## About Pal Karma Zurmang Shedup Chokhor Ling Lingdum Monastery (Ranka Monastery)

Pal Karma Zurmang Shedup Chokhor Ling Lingdum Monastery (Ranka Monastery) is a popular **Spiritual** destination located in **Sikkim**, Northeast India. Categorized as *Buddhist Monastery*, it draws visitors looking for an authentic Northeast Indian experience.

## Entry Fees

| Visitor Type | Fee |
|---|---|
| Indian Nationals | Free |
| Foreign Nationals | Free |

> Photography may have additional charges inside the premises.

## How to Reach

- **By Air:** Pakyong Airport, Gangtok (PYG) — approximately 15 km (26 mins drive)
- **By Air:** Bagdogra Airport (IXB) — approximately 105 km (180 mins drive)
- **By Train:** New Jalpaiguri Railway Station (NJP) — approximately 98 km (168 mins drive)

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **March, April, May, October, November, December**.

### Events & Festivals

- Losar (Feb-Mar)
- Saga Dawa (May-Jun)
- Pang Lhabsol (Aug-Sep)

## Visiting Hours

- **Opens:** 05:00 AM
- **Closes:** 06:00 PM
- **Note:** May close briefly in afternoon for rituals. Dress modestly.

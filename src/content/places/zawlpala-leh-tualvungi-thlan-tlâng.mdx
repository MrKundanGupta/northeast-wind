## About Zawlpala leh Tualvungi Thlan Tlâng

Zawlpala leh Tualvungi Thlan Tlâng is a popular **Heritage** destination located in **Mizoram**, Northeast India. Categorized as *Historical Monument*, it draws visitors looking for an authentic Northeast Indian experience.

## Entry Fees

| Visitor Type | Fee |
|---|---|
| Indian Nationals | ₹20 - ₹50 |
| Foreign Nationals | ₹200 - ₹500 |

## How to Reach

- **By Air:** Lengpui Airport, Aizawl (AJL) — approximately 59 km (101 mins drive)
- **By Train:** Bairabi Railway Station — approximately 137 km (235 mins drive)

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **October, November, December, January, February, March**.

### Events & Festivals

- Chapchar Kut (Mar)
- Mim Kut (Aug-Sep)
- Anthurium Festival (Sep)

## Permit Requirements

Inner Line Permit (ILP) required for Indian citizens. Apply online or at Liaison Officer offices in Kolkata, Delhi, Guwahati, or Silchar. Foreign nationals need a Restricted Area Permit.

## Visiting Hours

- **Opens:** 09:00 AM
- **Closes:** 05:00 PM
- **Closed on:** Monday
- **Note:** Some heritage sites close on national holidays.

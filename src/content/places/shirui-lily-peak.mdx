## About Shirui Lily Peak

Shirui Lily Peak is the only place on Earth where the Shirui lily (Lilium macklineae) grows in the wild. This delicate pink-and-white bell-shaped flower blooms each April–May on the grassy slopes of Shirui Hill near Ukhrul, attracting botanists and nature lovers. The Shirui Lily Festival celebrates this unique natural heritage every year.

- **From Ukhrul:** Approximately 12 km (25 minutes by road)

## About Sela Lake

Sela Lake is a popular **Lake / Nature** destination located in **Arunachal Pradesh**, Northeast India. Categorized as *Lake*, it draws visitors looking for an authentic Northeast Indian experience.

## Entry Fees

| Visitor Type | Fee |
|---|---|
| Indian Nationals | Free / ₹50 |
| Foreign Nationals | Free / ₹100 |

## How to Reach

- **By Air:** Donyi Polo Airport, Itanagar (HGI) — approximately 227 km (389 mins drive)
- **By Air:** Tezpur Airport (TEZ) — approximately 156 km (267 mins drive)
- **By Train:** Naharlagun Railway Station — approximately 228 km (391 mins drive)

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **October, November, December, January, February, March, April**.

### Events & Festivals

- Losar Festival (Feb-Mar)
- Torgya Festival (Jan)
- Ziro Music Festival (Sep)

## Permit Requirements

Inner Line Permit (ILP) required for Indian citizens. Protected Area Permit (PAP) required for foreign nationals. Apply online at arunachalilp.com or through registered tour operators.

## Visiting Hours

- **Opens:** 06:00 AM
- **Closes:** 06:00 PM
- **Note:** Boating hours may differ. Best visited in early morning or late afternoon.

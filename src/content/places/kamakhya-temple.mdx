## About Kamakhya Temple

Kamakhya Temple is one of the 51 Shakti Peethas and the most sacred tantric shrine in India, perched atop Nilachal Hill overlooking Guwahati. Millions of devotees visit for its unique worship of the goddess in her menstrual form — no idol, just a yoni-shaped stone bathed by an underground spring.

## How to Reach

- **From Guwahati:** Approximately 8 km (22 minutes by road)

## About Seven Sisters Falls

Seven Sisters Falls — locally called Nohsngithiang — is a group of seven parallel waterfalls that cascade down a cliff face during the monsoon, creating a spectacular curtain of white water. The falls are visible from the Eco Park viewpoint in Sohra and only flow fully after heavy rainfall, making a monsoon visit essential.

## How to Reach

- **From Cherrapunji:** Approximately 10 km (20 minutes by road)


## Best Time to Visit

Ideal months: **June, July, August, September, October**

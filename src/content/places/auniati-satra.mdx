## About Auniati Satra

Auniati Satra is the wealthiest and most influential monastery on Majuli, housing an extraordinary museum of royal gifts — ancient weapons, jewellery, utensils, and manuscripts gifted by Ahom kings over centuries. The satra remains a living religious community where monks maintain Vaishnavite traditions of music, dance and philosophy.

## About Thasiama Seno-neihna

Thasiama Seno-neihna is a popular **Tourist Attraction** destination located in **Mizoram**, Northeast India. Categorized as *Sightseeing*, it draws visitors looking for an authentic Northeast Indian experience.

## How to Reach

- **By Air:** Lengpui Airport, Aizawl (AJL) — approximately 62 km (106 mins drive)
- **By Train:** Bairabi Railway Station — approximately 139 km (238 mins drive)

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **October, November, December, January, February, March**.

### Events & Festivals

- Chapchar Kut (Mar)
- Mim Kut (Aug-Sep)
- Anthurium Festival (Sep)

## Permit Requirements

Inner Line Permit (ILP) required for Indian citizens. Apply online or at Liaison Officer offices in Kolkata, Delhi, Guwahati, or Silchar. Foreign nationals need a Restricted Area Permit.

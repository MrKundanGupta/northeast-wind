## About Kyongnosla Alpine Sanctuary

Kyongnosla Alpine Sanctuary is a popular **Wildlife & Nature** destination located in **Sikkim**, Northeast India. Categorized as *Wildlife Sanctuary*, it draws visitors looking for an authentic Northeast Indian experience.

## Entry Fees

| Visitor Type | Fee |
|---|---|
| Indian Nationals | ₹100 - ₹500 |
| Foreign Nationals | ₹500 - ₹2000 |

> Separate charges for camera, vehicle, and guide. Prices vary by zone and vehicle type.

## How to Reach

- **By Air:** Pakyong Airport, Gangtok (PYG) — approximately 32 km (55 mins drive)
- **By Air:** Bagdogra Airport (IXB) — approximately 122 km (209 mins drive)
- **By Train:** New Jalpaiguri Railway Station (NJP) — approximately 112 km (192 mins drive)

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **March, April, May, October, November, December**.

### Events & Festivals

- Losar (Feb-Mar)
- Saga Dawa (May-Jun)
- Pang Lhabsol (Aug-Sep)

## Visiting Hours

- **Opens:** 06:00 AM
- **Closes:** 04:30 PM
- **Closed on:** Varies by season
- **Note:** Entry may close 1-2 hours before official closing. Closed during monsoon in some parks.

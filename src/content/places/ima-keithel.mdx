## About Ima Keithel

Ima Keithel — 'Mother's Market' — is the world's largest all-women market, operating for over 500 years in the heart of Imphal. Over 5,000 women traders sell everything from handloom fabrics and fish to spices and bronze. The market is a cornerstone of Meitei culture and women's economic power, and visiting it is among the most extraordinary cultural experiences in Northeast India.

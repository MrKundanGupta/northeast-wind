## About Pilak Historical Stupa (Shyam Sundar Tila)

Pilak Historical Stupa (Shyam Sundar Tila) is a popular **Heritage** destination located in **Tripura**, Northeast India. Categorized as *Historical Monument*, it draws visitors looking for an authentic Northeast Indian experience.

## Entry Fees

| Visitor Type | Fee |
|---|---|
| Indian Nationals | ₹20 - ₹50 |
| Foreign Nationals | ₹200 - ₹500 |

## How to Reach

- **By Air:** Maharaja Bir Bikram Airport, Agartala (IXA) — approximately 114 km (195 mins drive)
- **By Train:** Agartala Railway Station — approximately 106 km (182 mins drive)

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **October, November, December, January, February, March**.

### Events & Festivals

- Kharchi Puja (Jul)
- Garia Puja (Apr)
- Orange Festival (Nov)

- **Opens:** 09:00 AM
- **Closes:** 05:00 PM

## About Tripura State Museum

Tripura State Museum is a popular **Museum** destination located in **Tripura**, Northeast India. Categorized as *Museum*, it draws visitors looking for an authentic Northeast Indian experience.

## Entry Fees

| Visitor Type | Fee |
|---|---|
| Indian Nationals | ₹20 - ₹50 |
| Foreign Nationals | ₹100 - ₹300 |

## How to Reach

- **By Air:** Maharaja Bir Bikram Airport, Agartala (IXA) — approximately 9 km (15 mins drive)
- **By Train:** Agartala Railway Station — approximately 1 km (10 mins drive)

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **October, November, December, January, February, March**.

### Events & Festivals

- Kharchi Puja (Jul)
- Garia Puja (Apr)
- Orange Festival (Nov)

## Visiting Hours

- **Opens:** 10:00 AM
- **Closes:** 04:30 PM
- **Closed on:** Monday, National Holidays
- **Note:** Last entry typically 30 mins before closing.

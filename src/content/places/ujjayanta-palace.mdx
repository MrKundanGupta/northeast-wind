## About Ujjayanta Palace

Ujjayanta Palace is a popular **Heritage** destination located in **Tripura**, Northeast India. Categorized as *Historical Monument*, it draws visitors looking for an authentic Northeast Indian experience.

## Entry Fees

| Visitor Type | Fee |
|---|---|
| Indian Nationals | ₹20 - ₹50 |
| Foreign Nationals | ₹200 - ₹500 |

## How to Reach

- **By Air:** Maharaja Bir Bikram Airport, Agartala (IXA) — approximately 9 km (15 mins drive)
- **By Train:** Agartala Railway Station — approximately 1 km (10 mins drive)

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **October, November, December, January, February, March**.

- Garia Puja (Apr)

## About Kakochang Waterfall

Kakochang Waterfall cascades through a lush tea-garden landscape near Bokakhat, making it one of Assam's most scenic waterfalls. Surrounded by rolling tea bushes and dense jungle, the falls are at their most dramatic during the monsoon season when water thunders down multiple tiers.

## How to Reach

- **From Kaziranga:** Approximately 25 km (40 minutes by road)


## Best Time to Visit

Ideal months: **June, July, August, September, October, November**

## About Jorhat

Jorhat is Assam's tea capital, surrounded by over 100 tea gardens and rich with Vaishnavite heritage. It's the gateway to Majuli, the world's largest river island, and home to the rare Hoolock Gibbon.

## Top Attractions Near Jorhat

- **Tea Estate Visits**
- **Gibbon Wildlife Sanctuary**
- **Thengal Bhawan**
- **Dhekiakhowa Bornamghar**
- **Majuli Ferry**
- **Tocklai Tea Research Station**

## Why Visit Jorhat

Tea capital of Assam with over 100 tea gardens; jump-off for Majuli river island.

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **October, November, December, January, February, March**.

## How to Reach Jorhat


- **By Air:** Direct or connecting flights from Delhi, Kolkata, and Guwahati.
- **By Rail:** Train connections to major Northeast railway stations.
- **By Road:** NH connections from Guwahati and neighbouring states.

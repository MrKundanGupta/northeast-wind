## About Mechuka

Mechuka is the remotest valley in Arunachal — a pristine Himalayan bowl near the Tibet border where the Yargyap Chu river gleams silver, ancient monasteries stand in silence, and the Memba tribe preserves a world unchanged.

## Top Attractions Near Mechuka

- **Mechuka Valley Scenery**
- **Yargyap Chu River**
- **Samten Yangchag Monastery**
- **Dorjeeling Village**
- **Mechuka Adventure Festival**
- **Local Memba Culture**

## Why Visit Mechuka

Offbeat high-valley hub near the Indo-Tibet border.

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **April, May, September, October**.

## How to Reach Mechuka


- **By Air:** Nearest airport with connecting flights from Guwahati and Kolkata.
- **By Road:** Well-connected by NH from Guwahati and state capitals.

## Permit Requirements

Inner Line Permit (ILP) required for Indian citizens. Foreign nationals require a Protected Area Permit (PAP). Apply online at the official state government portal well in advance.

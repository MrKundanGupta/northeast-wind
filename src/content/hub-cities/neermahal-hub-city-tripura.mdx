## About Neermahal

Neermahal rises from the middle of Rudrasagar Lake like a Mughal mirage — Tripura's stunning water palace, built in 1930, glowing golden at sunset. One of the most unique royal monuments in all of Northeast India.

## Top Attractions Near Neermahal

- **Neermahal Water Palace**
- **Rudrasagar Lake**
- **Sepahijala Wildlife Sanctuary**
- **Tepania Eco Park**
- **Boat Rides at Sunset**

## Why Visit Neermahal

Lake and water palace hub; easy day/overnight trip from Agartala.

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **October, November, December, January, February, March**.

## How to Reach Neermahal


- **By Air:** Direct or connecting flights from Delhi, Kolkata, and Guwahati.
- **By Rail:** Train connections to major Northeast railway stations.
- **By Road:** NH connections from Guwahati and neighbouring states.

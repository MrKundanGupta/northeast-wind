## About Aizawl

Aizawl is Mizoram's vibrant capital — a vertical city built on steep hills, crowned by the towering Solomon's Temple. Explore its lively bazaars, hilltop views over the Tlawng River, and the warm Christian Mizo culture.

## Top Attractions Near Aizawl

- **Solomon's Temple**
- **Durtlang Hills**
- **Mizoram State Museum**
- **Bara Bazar Market**
- **Aizawl Zoo**
- **Hmuifang Hill Resort**
- **Reiek Tlang Trek**

## Why Visit Aizawl

Capital and primary gateway; central base for city sights and day trips.

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **October, November, December, January, February, March**.

## How to Reach Aizawl


- **By Air:** Nearest airport with connecting flights from Guwahati and Kolkata.
- **By Road:** Well-connected by NH from Guwahati and state capitals.

## Permit Requirements

Inner Line Permit (ILP) required for Indian citizens. Foreign nationals require a Protected Area Permit (PAP). Apply online at the official state government portal well in advance.

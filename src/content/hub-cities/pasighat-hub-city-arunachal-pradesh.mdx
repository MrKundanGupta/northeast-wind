## About Pasighat

Pasighat, Arunachal's oldest town, sits where the mighty Siang River enters the plains. A paradise for river rafting, wildlife spotting in Daying Ering Sanctuary, and exploring the rich Adi tribal culture.

## Top Attractions Near Pasighat

- **Siang Riverfront**
- **Hanging Bridges**
- **Daying Ering Wildlife Sanctuary**
- **Kekar Monying Waterfall**
- **Craft Village**
- **River Rafting**

## Why Visit Pasighat

Oldest town in Arunachal; base on the Siang river.

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **October, November, December, January, February, March**.

## How to Reach Pasighat


- **By Air:** Nearest airport with connecting flights from Guwahati and Kolkata.
- **By Road:** Well-connected by NH from Guwahati and state capitals.

## Permit Requirements

Inner Line Permit (ILP) required for Indian citizens. Foreign nationals require a Protected Area Permit (PAP). Apply online at the official state government portal well in advance.

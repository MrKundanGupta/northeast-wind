## About Bomdila

Bomdila is the acclimatisation stop on the road to Tawang — a hill town of Buddhist gompas, apple orchards, and sweeping Himalayan views that prepare travellers for the high-altitude journey ahead.

## Top Attractions Near Bomdila

* **Bomdila Monastery**
* **Upper Gompa**
* **Middle Gompa**
* **Valley Viewpoints**
* **War Memorial**
* **Craft Centre**
* **Apple Orchards**

## Why Visit Bomdila

Acclimatisation hub on the western circuit between Tezpur and Tawang.

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **March, April, May, October, November**.

## How to Reach Bomdila

* **By Air:** Nearest airport with connecting flights from Guwahati and Kolkata.
* **By Road:** Well-connected by NH from Guwahati and state capitals.

## Permit Requirements

Inner Line Permit (ILP) required for Indian citizens. Foreign nationals require a Protected Area Permit (PAP). Apply online at the official state government portal well in advance.

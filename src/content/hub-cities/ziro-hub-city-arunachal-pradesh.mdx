## About Ziro

Ziro Valley is Arunachal's most poetic landscape — a UNESCO-nominated valley of terraced paddy fields, pine forests, and Apatani villages where women wear traditional face tattoos. Every September, it hosts the legendary Ziro Music Festival.

## Top Attractions Near Ziro

- **Apatani Village Circuit**
- **Ziro Valley Paddy Fields**
- **Talley Valley Wildlife Sanctuary**
- **Kardo Hill**
- **Ziro Putu Viewpoint**
- **Ziro Music Festival (September)**

## Why Visit Ziro

Cultural and landscape hub of the Apatani people; Ziro Music Festival base.

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **March, April, May, September, October**.

## How to Reach Ziro


- **By Air:** Nearest airport with connecting flights from Guwahati and Kolkata.
- **By Road:** Well-connected by NH from Guwahati and state capitals.

## Permit Requirements

Inner Line Permit (ILP) required for Indian citizens. Foreign nationals require a Protected Area Permit (PAP). Apply online at the official state government portal well in advance.

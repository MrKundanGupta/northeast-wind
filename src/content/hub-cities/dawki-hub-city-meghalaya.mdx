## About Dawki

Dawki sits on the Umngot River — so clear you can see every pebble on the riverbed. Boat through this natural aquarium, camp at Shnongpdeng, and gaze across into Bangladesh from the hilltops above.

## Top Attractions Near Dawki

- **Umngot River Boating**
- **Crystal Clear Water Views**
- **Shnongpdeng Camping**
- **Kayaking and Swimming**
- **India-Bangladesh Border Viewpoint**

## Why Visit Dawki

River-side adventure hub on the crystal-clear Umngot River.

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **October, November, December, January, February, March**.

## How to Reach Dawki


- **By Air:** Direct or connecting flights from Delhi, Kolkata, and Guwahati.
- **By Rail:** Train connections to major Northeast railway stations.
- **By Road:** NH connections from Guwahati and neighbouring states.

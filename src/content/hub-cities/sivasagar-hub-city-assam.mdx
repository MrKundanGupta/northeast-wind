## About Sivasagar

Sivasagar was the capital of the Ahom kingdom for 600 years. Explore extraordinary medieval monuments including Rang Ghar (Asia's oldest amphitheatre), the Talatal Ghar palace, and sacred Sivadol temples.

## Top Attractions Near Sivasagar

- **Rang Ghar**
- **Talatal Ghar**
- **Sivadol Temples**
- **Ahom Museum**
- **Sivasagar Lake**
- **Joysagar Lake**
- **Charaideo Maidam**

## Why Visit Sivasagar

Historic Ahom capital with remarkable medieval architecture and heritage.

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **October, November, December, January, February**.

## How to Reach Sivasagar


- **By Air:** Direct or connecting flights from Delhi, Kolkata, and Guwahati.
- **By Rail:** Train connections to major Northeast railway stations.
- **By Road:** NH connections from Guwahati and neighbouring states.

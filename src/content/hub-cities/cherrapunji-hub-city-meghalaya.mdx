## About Cherrapunji

Cherrapunji — also called Sohra — is one of the wettest places on Earth. Marvel at the thundering Nohkalikai Falls, explore limestone caves, and trek to living root bridges in the cloud-covered khasi hills.

## Top Attractions Near Cherrapunji

- **Nohkalikai Falls**
- **Seven Sisters Falls**
- **Wei Sawdong Waterfall**
- **Mawsmai Cave**
- **Arwah Cave**
- **Eco Park**
- **Garden of Caves**
- **Bangladesh Viewpoints**

## Why Visit Cherrapunji

Classic nature and waterfall hub; base for caves and living root bridges.

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **June, July, August, September, October**.

## How to Reach Cherrapunji


- **By Air:** Direct or connecting flights from Delhi, Kolkata, and Guwahati.
- **By Rail:** Train connections to major Northeast railway stations.
- **By Road:** NH connections from Guwahati and neighbouring states.

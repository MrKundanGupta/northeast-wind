## About Shillong

Shillong is the 'Scotland of the East' — a vibrant hill capital with misty lakes, colonial-era charm, indie music culture, and dramatic canyon views. The essential base for exploring all of Meghalaya.

## Top Attractions Near Shillong

- **Umiam Lake**
- **Shillong Peak**
- **Elephant Falls**
- **Ward's Lake**
- **Don Bosco Museum**
- **Laitlum Canyons**
- **Police Bazar Markets**
- **Cherrapunji Day Trip**

## Why Visit Shillong

Capital city and primary base for all of Meghalaya; 2–4 nights recommended.

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **October, November, December, January, February, March, April**.

## How to Reach Shillong


- **By Air:** Direct or connecting flights from Delhi, Kolkata, and Guwahati.
- **By Rail:** Train connections to major Northeast railway stations.
- **By Road:** NH connections from Guwahati and neighbouring states.

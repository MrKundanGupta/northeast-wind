## About Lachen

Lachen is the stepping stone to the sacred Gurudongmar Lake at 17,800 ft — one of the highest lakes in the world. A village of yaks, rhododendron forests, and silence at the edge of the Tibetan Plateau.

## Top Attractions Near Lachen

- **Gurudongmar Lake**
- **Chopta Valley**
- **Thangu Valley**
- **Shingba Rhododendron Sanctuary**
- **Lachen Monastery**
- **High Altitude Meadows**

## Why Visit Lachen

High-altitude base for Gurudongmar Lake and North Sikkim wilderness.

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **March, April, May, October**.

## How to Reach Lachen


- **By Air:** Direct or connecting flights from Delhi, Kolkata, and Guwahati.
- **By Rail:** Train connections to major Northeast railway stations.
- **By Road:** NH connections from Guwahati and neighbouring states.

## Permit Requirements

Inner Line Permit (ILP) required for Indian citizens. Foreign nationals require a Protected Area Permit (PAP). Apply online at the official state government portal well in advance.

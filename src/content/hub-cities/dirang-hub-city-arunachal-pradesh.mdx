## About Dirang

Dirang Valley is a hidden gem between Bomdila and Tawang — a gentle paradise of hot springs, kiwi orchards, ancient dzongs, and the serene Sangti Valley where black-necked cranes winter by the river.

## Top Attractions Near Dirang

- **Dirang Dzong**
- **Thupsung Dhargye Ling Monastery**
- **Sangti Valley**
- **Black-Necked Crane Habitat**
- **Hot Springs**
- **Yak Research Centre**
- **Kiwi and Apple Orchards**

## Why Visit Dirang

Scenic valley hub with birding, orchards and relaxed stays before Tawang.

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **October, November, December, March, April, May**.

## How to Reach Dirang


- **By Air:** Nearest airport with connecting flights from Guwahati and Kolkata.
- **By Road:** Well-connected by NH from Guwahati and state capitals.

## Permit Requirements

Inner Line Permit (ILP) required for Indian citizens. Foreign nationals require a Protected Area Permit (PAP). Apply online at the official state government portal well in advance.

## About Tura

Tura is the gateway to the Garo Hills — a world of dense forests, dramatic sandstone canyons at Balpakram, rare citrus fruits in Nokrek Biosphere, and the extraordinary Siju bat caves.

## Top Attractions Near Tura

- **Nokrek National Park**
- **Tura Peak**
- **Siju Caves**
- **Balpakram National Park**
- **Baghmara Orchid Garden**
- **Garo Heritage Sites**

## Why Visit Tura

Western Meghalaya eco-tourism hub in the Garo Hills.

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **October, November, December, January, February**.

## How to Reach Tura


- **By Air:** Direct or connecting flights from Delhi, Kolkata, and Guwahati.
- **By Rail:** Train connections to major Northeast railway stations.
- **By Road:** NH connections from Guwahati and neighbouring states.

## About Wokha

Wokha is Nagaland's secret birding paradise — every October, millions of Amur falcons roost at Doyang Reservoir on their migration to Africa, creating one of the world's greatest wildlife spectacles. Mount Tiyi watches over it all.

## Top Attractions Near Wokha

- **Mount Tiyi**
- **Doyang Reservoir**
- **Amur Falcon Roosting (October-November)**
- **Lotha Villages**
- **Handicrafts**
- **Valley Views**

## Why Visit Wokha

Scenic hill and lake hub, famous for Amur falcon roosting.

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **October, November, December, January, February**.

## How to Reach Wokha


- **By Air:** Nearest airport with connecting flights from Guwahati and Kolkata.
- **By Road:** Well-connected by NH from Guwahati and state capitals.

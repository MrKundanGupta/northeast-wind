## About Dimapur

Dimapur is Nagaland's commercial gateway — home to mysterious Kachari megalithic ruins, a thriving craft village, and the Rangapahar forest reserve. The essential transit point before heading to Kohima and beyond.

## Top Attractions Near Dimapur

- **Kachari Ruins**
- **Diezephe Craft Village**
- **Nagaland Zoological Park**
- **Rangapahar Reserve Forest**
- **Triple Falls**
- **Local Markets**

## Why Visit Dimapur

Main gateway with airport and railhead; logistics hub for the state.

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **October, November, December, January, February**.

## How to Reach Dimapur


- **By Air:** Nearest airport with connecting flights from Guwahati and Kolkata.
- **By Road:** Well-connected by NH from Guwahati and state capitals.

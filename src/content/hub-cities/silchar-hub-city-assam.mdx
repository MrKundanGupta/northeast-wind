## About Silchar

Silchar is the cultural heart of the Barak Valley, surrounded by rolling hills and rivers. Explore ancient Khaspur ruins, peaceful lakes, and use it as a launchpad for Mizoram and Manipur.

## Top Attractions Near Silchar

- **Khaspur Ruins**
- **Dolu Lake**
- **Maniharan Tunnel**
- **Barak Valley Views**
- **Shree Shree Kali Temple**
- **Phubala Lake**

## Why Visit Silchar

Main urban hub of southern Assam (Barak Valley), connecting to Mizoram and Manipur.

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **October, November, December, January, February**.

## How to Reach Silchar


- **By Air:** Direct or connecting flights from Delhi, Kolkata, and Guwahati.
- **By Rail:** Train connections to major Northeast railway stations.
- **By Road:** NH connections from Guwahati and neighbouring states.

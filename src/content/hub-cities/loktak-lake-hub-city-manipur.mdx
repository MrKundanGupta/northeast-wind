## About Loktak Lake

Loktak Lake is the largest freshwater lake in Northeast India — a surreal landscape of floating phumdis (biomass islands), home to the endangered Sangai dancing deer and the only floating national park on Earth.

## Top Attractions Near Loktak Lake

- **Loktak Lake Floating Islands (Phumdis)**
- **Sendra Island**
- **Keibul Lamjao National Park**
- **Sangai Deer Sanctuary**
- **INA Memorial at Moirang**
- **Sunset Boat Rides**

## Why Visit Loktak Lake

Signature lake hub with floating islands and rare Sangai deer.

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **October, November, December, January, February, March**.

## How to Reach Loktak Lake


- **By Air:** Nearest airport with connecting flights from Guwahati and Kolkata.
- **By Road:** Well-connected by NH from Guwahati and state capitals.

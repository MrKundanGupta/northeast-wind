## About Udaipur

Udaipur in Tripura is a sacred town centred on the Tripura Sundari Temple — one of the 51 Shakti Peethas and one of the most revered goddess shrines in India, set amid serene lakes and royal gardens.

## Top Attractions Near Udaipur

- **Tripura Sundari Temple**
- **Amarsagar Lake**
- **Dhanyashri Lake**
- **Bhuvaneshwari Temple**
- **Matsya Bhavan Aquarium**
- **Coconut Groves**

## Why Visit Udaipur

Historic temple and lake hub further south.

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **October, November, December, January, February**.

## How to Reach Udaipur


- **By Air:** Direct or connecting flights from Delhi, Kolkata, and Guwahati.
- **By Rail:** Train connections to major Northeast railway stations.
- **By Road:** NH connections from Guwahati and neighbouring states.

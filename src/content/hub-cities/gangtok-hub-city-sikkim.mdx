## About Gangtok

Gangtok is Sikkim's elegant hill capital — a cosmopolitan city with Buddhist monasteries, cable cars over mountain ridges, and breathtaking views of Kanchenjunga. The perfect base for North, South, East, and West Sikkim explorations.

## Top Attractions Near Gangtok

- **MG Marg Promenade**
- **Enchey Monastery**
- **Do-Drul Chorten**
- **Tashi View Point**
- **Rumtek Monastery**
- **Tsomgo Lake Day Trip**
- **Nathu La Pass Day Trip**
- **Banjhakri Falls**

## Why Visit Gangtok

Capital and primary base; almost every Sikkim trip starts here.

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **March, April, May, October, November, December**.

## How to Reach Gangtok


- **By Air:** Direct or connecting flights from Delhi, Kolkata, and Guwahati.
- **By Rail:** Train connections to major Northeast railway stations.
- **By Road:** NH connections from Guwahati and neighbouring states.

## About Tawang

Tawang is Arunachal Pradesh's crown jewel — home to one of the world's largest Buddhist monasteries, the sacred Sela Pass at 13,700 ft, the crystal Sela Lake, and a deeply moving war memorial from the 1962 conflict.

## Top Attractions Near Tawang

- **Tawang Monastery**
- **Tawang War Memorial**
- **Sela Pass**
- **Sela Lake**
- **Nuranang Falls**
- **Bumla Pass**
- **Madhuri Lake**
- **Penga Teng Tso Lake**

## Why Visit Tawang

Signature high-altitude hub — monasteries, lakes and war history.

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **March, April, May, June, September, October**.

## How to Reach Tawang


- **By Air:** Nearest airport with connecting flights from Guwahati and Kolkata.
- **By Road:** Well-connected by NH from Guwahati and state capitals.

## Permit Requirements

Inner Line Permit (ILP) required for Indian citizens. Foreign nationals require a Protected Area Permit (PAP). Apply online at the official state government portal well in advance.

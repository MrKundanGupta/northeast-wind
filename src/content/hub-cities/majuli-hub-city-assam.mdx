## About Majuli

Majuli is the world's largest river island — a living museum of Assamese culture where ancient Satras preserve centuries-old mask-making, dance, and Vaishnavite traditions surrounded by the mighty Brahmaputra.

## Top Attractions Near Majuli

- **Kamalabari Satra**
- **Auniati Satra**
- **Garamur Satra**
- **Dakhinpat Satra**
- **Samaguri Mask Making**
- **Birdwatching**
- **Brahmaputra Island Views**

## Why Visit Majuli

World's largest river island and neo-Vaishnavite cultural centre.

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **October, November, December, January, February**.

## How to Reach Majuli


- **By Air:** Direct or connecting flights from Delhi, Kolkata, and Guwahati.
- **By Rail:** Train connections to major Northeast railway stations.
- **By Road:** NH connections from Guwahati and neighbouring states.

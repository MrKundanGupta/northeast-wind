## About Ravangla

Ravangla in South Sikkim is a pilgrimage and panorama destination — home to the magnificent Tathagata Tsal Buddha Park, the world's tallest Guru Padmasambhava statue at Namchi, and Ralang Monastery's vibrant Kagyed dance festival.

## Top Attractions Near Ravangla

- **Buddha Park of Ravangla**
- **Samdruptse Hill (Guru Padmasambhava Statue)**
- **Namchi Char Dham**
- **Ralang Monastery**
- **Tea Gardens**
- **Valley Views**

## Why Visit Ravangla

South Sikkim hub with Buddha Park and monasteries.

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **October, November, December, January, February, March, April, May**.

## How to Reach Ravangla


- **By Air:** Direct or connecting flights from Delhi, Kolkata, and Guwahati.
- **By Rail:** Train connections to major Northeast railway stations.
- **By Road:** NH connections from Guwahati and neighbouring states.

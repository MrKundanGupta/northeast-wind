## About Haflong

Haflong is Assam's only hill station, perched in the Dima Hasao hills with cool air and sweeping views. Explore Haflong Lake, ancient ruins at Maibong, and the mysterious Jatinga valley.

## Top Attractions Near Haflong

- **Haflong Lake**
- **Haflong Viewpoints**
- **Maibong Ruins**
- **Ramchandi Temple**
- **Jatinga Valley**
- **Hill Forest Trails**

## Why Visit Haflong

Assam's only hill station with cool climate and panoramic views.

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **October, November, December, January, February, March**.

## How to Reach Haflong


- **By Air:** Direct or connecting flights from Delhi, Kolkata, and Guwahati.
- **By Rail:** Train connections to major Northeast railway stations.
- **By Road:** NH connections from Guwahati and neighbouring states.

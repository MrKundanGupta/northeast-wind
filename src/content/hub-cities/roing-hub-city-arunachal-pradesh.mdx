## About Roing

Roing is Arunachal's quiet eastern hub — base for the snowbound Mayudia Pass, the turquoise Mehao Lake, the ancient Bhismaknagar fort ruins, and rich Mishmi tribal culture deep in the Dibang Valley foothills.

## Top Attractions Near Roing

- **Mayudia Pass**
- **Mehao Lake**
- **Mehao Wildlife Sanctuary**
- **Bhismaknagar Fort Ruins**
- **Reh Festival**
- **Mishmi Hills Treks**

## Why Visit Roing

Eastern hub between plains and hills; base for lakes and archaeology.

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **October, November, December, January, February**.

## How to Reach Roing


- **By Air:** Nearest airport with connecting flights from Guwahati and Kolkata.
- **By Road:** Well-connected by NH from Guwahati and state capitals.

## Permit Requirements

Inner Line Permit (ILP) required for Indian citizens. Foreign nationals require a Protected Area Permit (PAP). Apply online at the official state government portal well in advance.

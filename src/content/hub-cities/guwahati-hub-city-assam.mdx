## About Guwahati

Guwahati is the gateway to Northeast India — a city of ancient temples, Brahmaputra river cruises, and vibrant markets. Home to the sacred Kamakhya Temple and a perfect base to explore Assam and beyond.

## Top Attractions Near Guwahati

* **Kamakhya Temple**
* **Umananda Island**
* **Brahmaputra River Cruise**
* **Deepor Beel Wildlife Sanctuary**
* **Pobitora Wildlife Sanctuary**
* **Hajo**
* **Sualkuchi Silk Village**

## Why Visit Guwahati

Largest city and main gateway for flights, trains and buses into Northeast India.

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **October, November, December, January, February, March**.

## How to Reach Guwahati

* **By Air:** Direct or connecting flights from Delhi, Kolkata, and Guwahati.
* **By Rail:** Train connections to major Northeast railway stations.
* **By Road:** NH connections from Guwahati and neighbouring states.

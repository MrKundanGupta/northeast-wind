## About Tinsukia

Tinsukia is the gateway to Dibru-Saikhowa National Park, famous for feral horses and river dolphins. Explore the historic Digboi oil town and the dense biodiversity of Bherjan-Borjan forests.

## Top Attractions Near Tinsukia

- **Dibru-Saikhowa National Park**
- **Bherjan-Borjan-Padumoni Wildlife Sanctuary**
- **Bell Temple**
- **Digboi Oil Town**
- **Stilwell Road History**

## Why Visit Tinsukia

Base for wilderness and heritage in far-eastern Assam.

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **November, December, January, February, March**.

## How to Reach Tinsukia


- **By Air:** Direct or connecting flights from Delhi, Kolkata, and Guwahati.
- **By Rail:** Train connections to major Northeast railway stations.
- **By Road:** NH connections from Guwahati and neighbouring states.

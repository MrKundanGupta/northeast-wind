## About Serchhip

Serchhip is home to Vantawng Falls — Mizoram's tallest waterfall — cascading through a forested gorge. Explore Thenzawl's famous handloom weaving village and the haunting historic memorial of Chhingpuii Thlan.

## Top Attractions Near Serchhip

- **Vantawng Falls**
- **Chhingpuii Thlan**
- **Thenzawl Village**
- **Thenzawl Handloom Centre**
- **Serchhip Town Views**

## Why Visit Serchhip

Waterfall and nature hub south of Aizawl.

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **October, November, December, January, February**.

## How to Reach Serchhip


- **By Air:** Nearest airport with connecting flights from Guwahati and Kolkata.
- **By Road:** Well-connected by NH from Guwahati and state capitals.

## Permit Requirements

Inner Line Permit (ILP) required for Indian citizens. Foreign nationals require a Protected Area Permit (PAP). Apply online at the official state government portal well in advance.

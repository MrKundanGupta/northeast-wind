## About Mon

Mon is Nagaland's most remote and fascinating district — home to the last headhunters of the Konyak Naga. Visit Longwa village where the chief's hut straddles India and Myanmar, and witness extraordinary tattooed warrior culture.

## Top Attractions Near Mon

- **Longwa Village (India-Myanmar Border)**
- **Chui Village**
- **Shangnyu Village**
- **Veda Peak**
- **Tattooed Headmen**
- **Aoleang Festival (April)**

## Why Visit Mon

Remote cultural hub of the Konyak Nagas near the India-Myanmar border.

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **October, November, December, January, February, March, April**.

## How to Reach Mon


- **By Air:** Nearest airport with connecting flights from Guwahati and Kolkata.
- **By Road:** Well-connected by NH from Guwahati and state capitals.

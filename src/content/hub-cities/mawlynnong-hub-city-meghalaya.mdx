## About Mawlynnong

Mawlynnong is Asia's cleanest village — a spotless Khasi hamlet near the Bangladesh border with bamboo sky views, a single-decker living root bridge, and a community that takes extraordinary pride in its environment.

## Top Attractions Near Mawlynnong

- **Mawlynnong Village**
- **Riwai Living Root Bridge**
- **Sky View Bamboo Tower**
- **Balancing Rock**
- **Bangladesh Border Viewpoint**
- **Village Nature Walks**

## Why Visit Mawlynnong

Asia's cleanest village near the Bangladesh border.

The ideal months to visit are **October, November, December, January, February, March**.

- **By Rail:** Train connections to major Northeast railway stations.
- **By Road:** NH connections from Guwahati and neighbouring states.

## About Nongriat

Nongriat is the trekking destination for the iconic Double Decker Living Root Bridge — a bio-engineering marvel grown over centuries by the Khasi people. The 3500-step descent rewards with crystal pools and rainforest.

## Top Attractions Near Nongriat

- **Double Decker Living Root Bridge**
- **Rainbow Falls**
- **Natural Rock Pool**
- **Deep Valley Trekking**
- **Homestay Village Walks**

## Why Visit Nongriat

Trekking hub for the legendary double-decker living root bridge.

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **October, November, December, January, February**.

## How to Reach Nongriat


- **By Air:** Direct or connecting flights from Delhi, Kolkata, and Guwahati.
- **By Rail:** Train connections to major Northeast railway stations.
- **By Road:** NH connections from Guwahati and neighbouring states.

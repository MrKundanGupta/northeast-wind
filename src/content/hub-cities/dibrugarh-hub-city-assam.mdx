## About Dibrugarh

Dibrugarh is Upper Assam's commercial heart, set amid endless tea gardens on the Brahmaputra. A gateway to Dibru-Saikhowa National Park and the pristine Dehing Patkai rainforest.

## Top Attractions Near Dibrugarh

- **Tea Garden Walks**
- **Brahmaputra Riverfront**
- **Dehing Patkai National Park**
- **Dibru-Saikhowa National Park**
- **Radhanagar Ghat**
- **Naharkatia Oil Fields**

## Why Visit Dibrugarh

Largest city of Upper Assam; airport gateway for eastern tea-country circuits.

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **October, November, December, January, February, March**.

## How to Reach Dibrugarh


- **By Air:** Direct or connecting flights from Delhi, Kolkata, and Guwahati.
- **By Rail:** Train connections to major Northeast railway stations.
- **By Road:** NH connections from Guwahati and neighbouring states.

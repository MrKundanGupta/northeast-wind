## About Kaziranga

Kaziranga is home to the world's largest population of one-horned rhinoceroses. Explore the UNESCO World Heritage National Park on jeep and elephant safaris, surrounded by lush tea gardens and misty hills.

## Top Attractions Near Kaziranga

- **Kaziranga National Park**
- **One-Horned Rhinoceros Safari**
- **Elephant Safari**
- **Tea Gardens**
- **Kakochang Waterfall**
- **Deopahar Ruins**
- **Numaligarh Refinery Heritage**

## Why Visit Kaziranga

Classic wildlife hub for safaris and tea garden exploration.

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **November, December, January, February, March, April**.

## How to Reach Kaziranga


- **By Air:** Direct or connecting flights from Delhi, Kolkata, and Guwahati.
- **By Rail:** Train connections to major Northeast railway stations.
- **By Road:** NH connections from Guwahati and neighbouring states.

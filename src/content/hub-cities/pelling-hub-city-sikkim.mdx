## About Pelling

Pelling offers the most dramatic face-to-face views of Kanchenjunga, the world's third-highest peak. Explore the ancient Pemayangtse Monastery, the ruins of Rabdentse (Sikkim's former capital), and the wish-granting Khecheopalri Lake.

## Top Attractions Near Pelling

- **Pemayangtse Monastery**
- **Rabdentse Ruins**
- **Khecheopalri Lake**
- **Kanchenjunga Views**
- **Khangchendzonga Waterfalls**
- **Helipad Viewpoint**
- **Rimbi River**

## Why Visit Pelling

Scenic Kanchenjunga-view hub and monastery-ruins circuit.

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit are **March, April, May, October, November**.

## How to Reach Pelling


- **By Air:** Direct or connecting flights from Delhi, Kolkata, and Guwahati.
- **By Rail:** Train connections to major Northeast railway stations.
- **By Road:** NH connections from Guwahati and neighbouring states.

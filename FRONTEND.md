# Design Principles for Northeast Stays
- **Visuals First:** Travel is emotional. Images must always have an aspect ratio of 4:3 or 16:9, use `object-cover`, and have subtle hover zoom effects (`hover:scale-105 duration-300`).
- **Whitespace is Luxury:** Use generous padding (e.g., `py-16` or `py-24` for sections). Never cram content.
- **Cards:** All listing cards must have `rounded-2xl`, a subtle border (`border-gray-100`), and a soft shadow (`shadow-sm hover:shadow-md`).
- **Typography:** Titles must be bold and high-contrast (`text-gray-900`). Meta-info should be subtle (`text-gray-500`).
